LOGGERHEADS

Event Date: 28 September 2016

Organiser


Prakyath Mathur

+91-9573621201

RULES AND REGULATION

  1. The topic will be given on the spot.
  2. The participants will be given 2 minutes to prepare a speech of 1 minute.
  3. The 4 participants will speak in an alternating fashion with a member of the team ‘FOR’ the notion speaking first followed by a member of the team ‘AGAINST’ the notion and so on. (F-A-F-A)
  4. The rebuttal will be for 5 minutes after the participants’ speeches.
  5. The participants will be judged based on the following criteria – Content, Diction, Intonation, Clarity in thought and speech and most importantly, team work. (Content – How relevant your points are to the topic Diction – Your usage of words and pronunciation Intonation – Voice modulation and how you convey emotions)
  6. Use of unparliamentary language will result in loss of points. Participants are expected to maintain proper conduct during the debate.
  7. Other general rules are applicable to all participants.
  8. The decision of the judging panel shall be final and binding.
Note:
Registration fee : 50/-
