  5. What do u see

    Rules

    What do u see

    RULES AND REGULATIONS

    a. There will be a Preliminary round and subsequent rounds.
    b. Participants will be given a letter and the challenge is to write as many words as possible with that letter in a minutes time.
    c. Each word will be scored based on the number of letters in it. Higher the number of letters more will be the score for that word.
    d. The number of subsequent rounds depends upon the number of participants.
    e. Other general rules are applicable to all participants.
    f. The decision of the judging panel shall be final and binding.
    Registration fee: Rs.50 per individual
