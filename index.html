    ABOUT US

    ISTE which stands for INDIAN SOCIETY OF TECHNICAL EDUCATION first started in 1941 as the Association of Principals of Technical Institutions (APTI), it was converted into "Indian Society for Technical Education" in 1968 with a view to enlarge its activities to advance the cause of technological education. The Ministry of Human Resource Development, CTE/DST/MIT/State Govts. are well associated with the ISTE for programs relating to technical education. Coming to ISTE-SNIST, Every student of SREENIDHI is a part of ISTE-SNIST.ISTE-SNIST provides a good platform for the students to improve technical knowledge in various fields.
    INNOVATION AND CREATIVITY CLUB An innovation is something original, novel, and important that obtains a foothold in a market or society. It refers to the notion of doing something different rather than doing the same thing better. Creativity, on the other hand, is characterized by originality, expressiveness, and imagination, a skill to improve using various methods. The skill can be creating an idea, a joke, an artistic or literary work, a painting or musical composition, a solution or even an invention. Having taken its birth on 26th Augustí13 as INNOVATION AND CREATIVITY CLUB at Sreenidhi Institute of Science and Technology, it has been putting on tremendous efforts in bringing out the creativity and Novel ideas out of the younger generation at SNIST. The Club makes an earnest attempt in encouraging the future Entrepreneurs of India and helping them out in moving a step closer to their goals. It runs on various platforms with an appetite to encourage the skill and creativity in every possible aspect. It bestows various opportunities to the students through the platforms such as such as Mayavi, Aavishkar, Laghu Chitra, Kala Srushti, Entrepreneurship, and Creative writing.
    Mirth was a spectacular cultural night since its birth and will be a one of that kind in 2016. Spiccato (a celebrity concert) and Feet-o-mania are the part of MIRTH.
